Frequently Asked Questions

DO COLLEGE PROVIDE ANY VOCATIONAL OR TRAINING COURSE FOR PLACEMENT?

These courses provide hands-on training and practical skills in specialized trades, leading to immediate job readiness and higher employment rates.

DOES THE COLLEGE PROVIDE ANY GUIDANCE/ASSISTANCE TO THE STUDENTS FOR PLACEMENTS?

The college provides guidance and assistance to students for placements through training programs, career counseling, resume building, mock interviews, industry interactions, a dedicated placement cell, internship opportunities, and an alumni network.

CAN A STUDENT SIT FOR PLACEMENTS AND HIGHER STUDIES BOTH AT THE SAME TIME?

Yes, a student can sit for placements and higher studies both at the same time. However, it depends on various factors such as the student's career goals, financial situation, and personal preferences.
